Vue Mobile Tags, Labels & Badges - Bootstrap 4 & Material Design

Vue Mobile tags are components which separate content placed in the same wrapper, but in the separate pane. Only one pane can be displayed at the time.

Their main goal is to provide your visitors with an intuitive way of getting what they want.
