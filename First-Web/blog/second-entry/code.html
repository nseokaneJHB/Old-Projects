How to set up a skateboard

At the very least skateboarding keeps you feeling young and healthy

ingredients

  • Board
  • Griptape
  • Trucks
  • Wheels
  • Bearings
  • Hardwera
  • Nuts
  • Tools

preparation

  1. Stick the griptape tape on the board
  2. Nicely cut the griptape on the side following the shape of the board
  3. Place the trucks under the board and tighten them with hardwera
  4. Insert the bearings inside the wheels, two bearings per wheel
  5. Place the wheels on the trucks and secure them with nuts
  6. Go skate for fun
